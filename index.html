Getting started

Install in
5 minutes

⚠️
FishAI is not yet on the Play Store. You need to allow installation from unknown sources. This is standard for beta apps and completely safe -- you can disable it again after installing.
  1. 1

    Allow Unknown Sources

    Go to Settings > Apps > find your browser > tap Install unknown apps > toggle ON. On some phones this is under Settings > Security.

  2. 2

    Download the APK

    Tap the download button at the top of this page on your Android phone. The file app-debug.apk will save to your Downloads folder.

  3. 3

    Install FishAI

    Open your Downloads folder, tap app-debug.apk, and tap Install. If prompted about unknown apps tap Allow.

  4. 4

    Allow Notifications & Location

    Tap Allow for both permissions when the app asks. Location powers the live weather. Notifications send hot bite alerts to your phone and watch.

  5. 5

    Select Species & Set Water Conditions

    Pick your target species, enter water temp, clarity, and structure, tap Update Strategy, and FishAI will tell you what to throw and when.
