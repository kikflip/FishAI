What's inside

Everything you need on the water

Built for anglers who want real intel, not generic advice.

📊
Live Bite Windows
Six time slots per day scored by weather, pressure, moon phase, temperature, and season. Know exactly when to be on the water.
🧠
AI Strategy Engine
Over 200 condition combinations drive lure picks, retrieve style, depth, and color. Adjusts for Beginner, Intermediate, and Not Biting modes.
🐟
3 Species Modes
Separate bite scoring and lure logic for Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, and Northern Pike. Switch species on the fly.
📅
7-Day Forecast
Plan your week with species-specific bite ratings for each day based on real forecast data from Open-Meteo.
📍
Catch Log + Map
Log weight, length, lure, depth, and photos. Every catch is pinned to a map so you can find your spots again.
🌐
Community Feed
See what other anglers are catching near you. Live leaderboard, catch map, and feed updated in real time.
🔔
Bite Notifications
Background worker checks conditions hourly and fires a notification when a hot bite window is approaching.
📱
Home Screen Widget
Glanceable bite percentage on your Android home screen. Know if it's worth heading out before you open the app.
🌊
Water Conditions Input
Enter water temp, depth, clarity, and nearby structure to sharpen the AI strategy for exactly where you're fishing.
