Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

FishAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the FishAI Android application.

1. Information We Collect

Location data. FishAI requests your device location to provide local weather conditions, bite window predictions, and to geotag catches you log. Location is used only while the app is in use and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Account information. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account name and email address. This is used to identify you on community features such as catch logs, leaderboards, and tournaments.

Catch log data. Catches you log (weight, length, lure, notes, photos, and GPS coordinates) are stored in your personal catch log. If you are signed in, community catches are shared to our Firebase database and visible to other users on the community map and feed.

Tournament data. If you create or join a tournament, your display name and catch submissions are stored in our Firebase database and visible to other tournament participants.

Photos. Photos you attach to catches are stored as compressed images in our Firebase database. They are visible to other users on the community feed.

Device information. We collect basic app version information to deliver over-the-air updates via GitHub. No device identifiers are collected or stored.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized bite window and lure recommendations based on your location and weather
  • To display your catches on the community map and feed
  • To power tournament leaderboards and catch logs
  • To notify you of hot bite windows via push notifications (only if you grant permission)
  • To deliver app updates

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase Realtime Database and Google Firebase Authentication, both of which are operated by Google LLC and subject to Google's security standards. We do not operate our own servers. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Third-Party Services

FishAI uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase -- authentication and data storage (Firebase Privacy)
  • Open-Meteo -- weather data, no personal data shared (Open-Meteo Terms)
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim -- reverse geocoding and maps (OSM Privacy)
  • USGS Water Services -- water temperature data, no personal data shared
  • Google Sign-In -- optional authentication (Google Privacy)
  • Google Play Billing -- in-app purchases processed by Google (Google Payments Privacy)

5. In-App Purchases

FishAI offers an optional one-time Pro unlock purchase processed through Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google. Purchase entitlements are stored in Firebase linked to your Google account UID.

6. Children's Privacy

FishAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

Community catch data and tournament data are retained indefinitely to maintain leaderboards and feeds. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Anonymous catch data submitted without a Google account is not linked to any individual and cannot be deleted on request.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via your device settings
  • Opt out of community catch sharing by using the app without signing in

9. Push Notifications

FishAI may send push notifications for hot bite windows if you grant notification permission. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings. We do not send marketing notifications.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app banner. Continued use of FishAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
